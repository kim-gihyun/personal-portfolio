# Hello, world

Welcome to my site. I built this to keep my CV in one place and to have somewhere
to write — research progress, project updates, and the occasional personal note.

## What you'll find here

- **Research progress reports** — short, regular updates on what I'm working on.
- **Project write-ups** — longer posts when something reaches a milestone.
- **Personal notes** — books, ideas, things I'm learning.

## Why a static site

No accounts, no database, no monthly fee. Just files. To publish a new post I write
a Markdown file and it shows up here. Simple enough that I'll actually keep doing it.
